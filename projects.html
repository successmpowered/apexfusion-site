LIVE · MAINNET Records · provenance

Genealogy · Serbian WWI Casualty Register

The first production pilot processed Serbia's WWI casualty register: handwritten scans that defeat every frontier model, run through paid, bonded inference on Vector mainnet. Every extracted fact carries a chain of custody: the source page it came from, the model that read it, and the AP3X payment that bought the reading. It is a proof case for the wider claim that AI output can be verified by an auditor.

// How to explore it
  1. Open the live site and browse records by soldier, unit, or date.
  2. Click any record to see its provenance chain: the scan, the model, and the receipt.
  3. Cross-check the receipt hash on the Vector mainnet explorer.
  4. Watch new records land in real time on the agent dashboard.
